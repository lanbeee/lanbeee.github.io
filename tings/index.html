Habits

A small place to notice what you want to build, limit, or stop. No account needed. Your entries stay on this device.

Start simple

Add a habit, select Build, Limit, or Stop, then tap its icon whenever it occurs. Double-tap to log it instantly. Tap Habits at the top any time to come back here or open settings.

Not a task list

A to-do list is for finishing one thing. This is for habits that repeat, fade, slip, or improve over time. You are watching the pattern, not clearing an inbox.

Not a calendar

A calendar is for exact appointments. This is lighter: you can plan a day, but the main goal is to notice rhythm, spacing, and progress.

Read the cards

The ring is the current state. The dash and dots show recent days. Green is good, yellow is close, red needs care, and purple is planned.

Use the calendar

Tap any date to see what happened, add something from the past, or plan something ahead.

Let order help

Pinned habits stay first. After that, auto order weighs plans, rhythm, recent entries, progress, trends, and habit type. Settings lets you pick a preset or fine tune it.

Open a habit

Tap a card for insight, calendar, and habit settings. Swipe across the page to move between them.

Quick actions

Tap the big icon or double tap a card to log. Undo appears after. Swipe right to pin or review activity. Swipe left to snooze or remove.

settings

Choose how the app behaves. These are app-wide settings, not settings for one habit.

home order

Pinned habits always stay first. After that, auto order brings up what needs attention.

priority preset

Presets move the controls below. Balanced watches everything, build favors routines, plans favors planned days, and today puts today items and overdue items first.

stop habits rise

Quiet keeps quit habits low. Watch gives resets a small nudge. Recent brings them into view. Strict treats resets as urgent.
