Habits

A small place to notice what you want to build, limit, or stop. No account needed. Your entries stay in this browser on this device.

Start simple

Add one habit, choose build, limit, or stop, then tap its icon when it happens. Tap Habits at the top any time to come back here or open settings.

Read the cards

The ring is the current state. The dash and dots show recent days. Green is good, yellow is close, red needs care, and purple is planned.

Use the calendar

Tap any date to see what happened, add something from the past, or plan something ahead.

Let order help

Pinned habits stay first. After that, auto order brings up what may need attention. Settings can make this calmer or more active.

Open a habit

Tap a card for insight, calendar, and habit settings. Swipe across the page to move between them.

Quick actions

Tap the big icon to log with confirmation. Double tap anywhere on a card to log quickly. Swipe right to pin or plan. Swipe left to snooze or remove.

settings

These shape how the home list decides what to show first.

auto order focus

Balanced is best for most people. Build favors due build habits. Space favors limit and stop habits.

sort weights

100 is normal. Lower means quieter. Higher means it rises sooner.

planned window

Controls how early planned habits can move up.
