how it works

Tings are recurring things you want to do more, do less, or stop. Each entry means it happened once.

+ button

Add a habit, or thing to track. Choose nurture, ease off, or quit.

top icon

Sort icon means smart order. List icon means hand order, and shows the drag grips for a little while.

tap icon

Record that it happened today with a confirmation.

double tap

Record today immediately when you do not need confirmation.

swipe left

Log something that happened before today, hide it for a week, or remove the ting.

hold grip

Drag a card to set your own order. The app will remember it.

tap card

Open details to see status, trend, graph, and settings for that ting.

tune

Change the icon and rhythm. Rhythm is the days you want between entries.

nurture

For things you want to keep doing. Due or nearly due tings move up.

ease off

For things you still do, just less often. Smart order keeps recent ones quieter.

quit

For things you want off the board. They stay low-key even after a slip.

gap

Days since the latest entry. If an old future entry exists, it shows days away.

pace

Your usual space between entries, based on recent history.

graph

Bars show entry spacing: green is good, amber is watch, red needs attention.
