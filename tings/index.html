how it works

A "habit" is something you want to keep doing, do less often, or stop. An entry means it happened once. A future entry is a plan.

log

Tap the big icon to log today with confirmation. Double tap a card to log quickly.

type

Build is for habits you want more of. Limit is for things you want less often. Stop is for things you want to avoid.

calendar

Tap a date to see what happened, add a past entry, or plan a future entry.

order

Smart order shows what needs attention. Custom order appears when you have more than four habits.

stats

Open a habit and swipe sideways between insight, calendar, and settings. Avg gap is your usual spacing. Planned counts future entries.

swipe

Swipe right to plan the next target date. Swipe left to snooze or remove.
